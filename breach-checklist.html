Step-by-step recovery playbook

  1. Change your password and enable 2FA immediately.
  2. Revoke suspicious sessions and tokens.
  3. Monitor financial accounts and set alerts.
  4. Rotate other accounts using similar credentials.
  5. Consider a credit freeze if PII was exposed.
