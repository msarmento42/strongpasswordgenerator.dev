Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use strongpasswordgenerator.dev. Our password and passphrase generators are designed to run entirely in your browser.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any passwords or passphrases you generate.
  • We do not track your generator settings (length, character sets, word count, etc.).
  • We do not require registration or personal information to use our generators.

How Generation Works

All password/passphrase generation happens locally in your browser using the Web Crypto API. Nothing you generate is sent to our servers.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to show ads. AdSense may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver, personalize, and measure ads. You can manage ad personalization in your Google Ad settings. Learn more in Google’s policies and your browser’s privacy controls.

Forms & Contact

If you submit a message via our Contact page, we will receive the information you provide (e.g., name, email, message) for the purpose of responding. Do not include sensitive data.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Review their policies before providing personal information.

Security

The site operates over HTTPS. Since generation is local, the security of your generated secrets also depends on the security of your device and browser. Keep your software up to date and consider using a reputable password manager.

Children’s Privacy

Our tools are general-audience utilities and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted to this page with an updated date.
