Compare Bitwarden, 1Password, and LastPass

Bitwarden

  • Price: Free core, low-cost premium
  • Open source: Yes
  • Sync: Yes
  • Pros: Affordable, transparent
  • Cons: UI less polished

1Password

  • Price: Paid plans only
  • Open source: No (proprietary)
  • Sync: Yes
  • Pros: Excellent UX, secure, travel mode
  • Cons: No free tier

LastPass

  • Price: Free basic, paid premium
  • Open source: No
  • Sync: Limited on free
  • Pros: Easy to use, broad support
  • Cons: Notable past security incidents

Summary

Bitwarden is great value and open source. 1Password has the slickest experience. LastPass is familiar but has a checkered history. Pick based on budget, transparency, and UX.
